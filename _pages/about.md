## About Me

**Understanding the physical origin of dark energy through data-driven reconstruction and scalar field dynamics.**

I am a postdoctoral researcher in cosmology at **ICF-UNAM**, focusing on the nature of dark energy and the dynamics of the late-time universe.

My work aims to connect **observationally reconstructed dark energy histories** with **physically motivated scalar field models**, providing a bridge between data and fundamental theory.

### Current Research Directions

- **Dark energy reconstruction from observational data**  
- **Scalar field and quintom dynamics**  
- **Cosmological tensions**: $H_0$ and $S_8$  

I combine observational datasets (DESI, Planck, Pantheon+) with theoretical modeling to understand the physical origin of dark energy.

## Research Interests

- Late-time cosmology  
- Dark energy phenomenology  
- Scalar field reconstruction  
- Cosmological parameter estimation  
- Observational tensions ($H_0$, $S_8$)
